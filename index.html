Section 01 — Client Intelligence
CLIENT
STRATEGY

Three confirmed clients across the Eastern Caribbean. Each has a distinct audience, communication challenge, and creative need. Teams study this before Day 3. Walk into every client meeting knowing more about their world than they expect.

Roseau, Dominica
Consulting

A full-service business consultancy with 15+ years of experience in BPO, HR solutions, logistics, and professional development. They've grown beyond their boutique origins but their digital presence hasn't caught up. They need the world to see who they've become.

Core challenge

Need more international and regional recognition. Their expertise is deep but their story is untold outside Dominica.

Brand Story Corporate Profile Social Media Cinematic Human-Centered

  • Who is your ideal client — paint us a picture of them

  • Do you have video testimonials or are interviews needed?
  • What has been your most transformational client success story?
🇦🇬
Digicel Group
Antigua & Barbuda
Telecom

Everyone knows Digicel for mobile. The challenge: corporate clients don't yet see Digicel as a one-stop enterprise solutions provider. They need to shift perception — from consumer telecoms to serious B2B partner offering cybersecurity, managed services, vehicle tracking, and more.

Core challenge

Corporate clients see Digicel as a phone company. The video must reframe them as a credible enterprise technology partner.

B2B Corporate Product Explainer Cybersecurity Premium Tone TV Delivery

  • Are there existing product demo videos or screenshots we can incorporate?

Grenada's tax authority trying to make compliance feel relevant — not threatening — to a broad public including young people. They want motion graphics, animation, and social reels that actually get watched.
